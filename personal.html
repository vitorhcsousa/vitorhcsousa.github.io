About Me

I am 26 years old, from Vizela, a small town in the north of Portugal.

I really enjoy learning and I always fight for my goals. Being a student-worker during the 5 years of University demanded a lot from me and helped me grow and develop beyond the academic point of view. I think it was the period of my life that shaped me the most, because besides university and studies I always tried to make sure that I would have time for the important things in life - my family, girlfriend (now wife) and my friends.
I am very proud of my path, it was quite hard but being financially independent at 18 and being able to pay for my course and all the expenses that anyone has is one of the things I am most proud of.

I think my parents and brother have always contributed a lot to this, we have always been very hard working and we were always clear that with hard work everything can be achieved. believe that we never stop learning, and especially in technological areas we always have to acquire new knowledge. So even today I try to dedicate some time of my week to deepen or acquire more knowledge.
