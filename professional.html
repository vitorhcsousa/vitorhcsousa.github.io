Pinto Brasil Group

My journey in Pinto Brasil Group was divided into 2 moments. A summer internship, combined with work at Idioma de Tons, and then full-time.

Summer Internship - Web Developer

Summer internship, from 15h to 20h, conciliated with my work at Idioma de Tons from 06-14h.
During this period, I worked as a web developer on web solutions that the Research and Development department was developing. I integrated a team with 4 developers and during this period I worked on a few projects for web interfaces of the products that the department developed.

Technologies: JavaScript, JQuery, PHP, HTML5, AJAX, CSS

Software Developer

At the end of the summer internship and I received an offer to work as a Software Developer in the R&D department, which I accepted. My work here was divided into 2 main parts. A project to develop an idea management solution, an investment and development project.

Idea Management

An idea management software to be able to follow up all suggestions from employees or customers, and that the whole process, until the creation of the project, if it was the case, was managed in this software. As a company with thousands of employees, and with some customers with very particular needs and requirements this was and is a tool to define which projects the department should start.

Technologies: JavaScript, JQuery, PHP, HTML5, AJAX, CSS,Angular, TypeScript and C#.

Research project - Prediction and identification of the moments of failure of the products of the department.

Together with the electronics and mechanics team, we developed a project that through the analysis of sensor data (temperature, humidity, etc.), operating history, etc... we tried to understand which are the failure moments and try to understand the reasons for these failures. Since the products(industrial machines) of the R&D department were new and in constant development until the incorporation of the product as a standard product of the company it was important to understand what are the possible improvements to get the most robust product possible.

Technologies: Python, Java, Mongo DB, talend, Power BI

Valuekeep - PRIMAVERA BSS

Here too, my path was divided into two distinct phases. As a software engineer and then, associated with the evolution of my academic career and my interest in the area, as a Data Scientist and Engineer

Software Engineer

The big project was the creation and development of a web solution for maintenance management. The "old" product was on outdated technologies, and I was the first member of the team to develop our solution on newer technologies that were more responsive to our customers. Besides developing the product, I was also involved in the rest of the product lifecycle, so I worked with the entire Azure environment to ensure best practices and CI/CD implementations. The technological basis of the solution is .NET/.NET Core (C#), SQL, EntityFramework, and Angular.

Data Scientist & Engineer

The goal of the project is to develop a product that uses Machine Learning techniques to offer a Predictive Maintenance solution.
In this project, I was included in two teams, the Data Engineering team and the Data Science and Machine Learning team. This project has the participation of the Computer Graphics Centre (CCG), from the University of Minho, and I was the most technical contact point between Valuekeep and the CCG teams.
In the Data Engineering, I was part of the micro-service responsible for the capture and storage of data in Azure Data Lake. I also developed pipelines for data transformation and processing, mainly using Azure Data Factory and Databricks.
Regarding the Machine Learning and Data Science team, we developed a set of descriptive, exploratory, and causal analyses to data. We also explored Machine Learning techniques for two distinct objectives, the prediction of the number of time units until the next asset failure (regression problem) and the probability of asset failure in a given time window (binary classification). In this development, the Databricks tool was used.

All the developments were done using Python, I also used PySpark. In terms of libraries, the most used were the following: pandas, NumPy, seaborn, matplotlib, sklearn.

Sonae MC

Academic Path
As I mentioned earlier, my entire academic path was reconciled with full-time work. This was a big challenge, especially in the first year of university, since I have not studied for two years, getting back to the academic routines was difficult.
This 5-year course has great versatility, there are a set of courses that allow us to have strong knowledge about a wide variety of subjects, operating systems, distributed systems, software development, economics and finance, a set of mathematical courses (calculus, linear algebra, statistical methods), etc. It is possible to orient and specify the syllabus to a specific area, especially in the master's degree, we can orient our study to very different areas. As we can select part of the curricular units we have the possibility to deepen our knowledge in the areas of most interest. In my case, I always chose curricular units oriented to data science, machine learning, data visualization, and big data.

Besides the technical skills, being a student worker allowed me to develop a set of other skills that are extremely important for me today. Soft skills like time and effort management, focus on the final result, discipline, my learning capacity was some of the points that I developed the most. I think I can optimize the way I acquire knowledge, and this was crucial for the course, but it's also very important now because in this world of data there is a constant evolution and being able to update myself and learn is one of the most important points so far in my career.

Despite being a student worker I am very proud of my entire path at university, I finished with an average of 15.4 marks. My master's thesis entitled "Artificial Intelligence for predictive maintenance" scored 17 points. Furthermore, the paper the paper developed based on this project won the Best IDEAL 2021 conference paper award.
